Courses

Courses that available for our SPM Students:

Bahasa Melayu

Bahasa Melayu is where SPM student will put to work in many kinds of exercise such as literature, essays and grammar. It also to helps student to be more fluent in malay language as it was the national language in Malaysia. with the help from our Experienced tutor, it will help student to be more prepared in their SPM Exam.

English

We will Prioritizes the key components of the English language, such as grammar, reading, listening, speaking, and writing, in order to help students improve their overall English language proficiency, which will help them in tertiary education and beyond. This will help students prepare for the SPM English examination.

Sejarah

Sejarah also known as History is one of subject that must be pass by student who take SPM examination. This courses will show to students on how to tackle the exam question properly from our tutor who was SPM examiner paper and the student will also get to know on how to get high marks in objective(MCQ) question.

Mathematics

Mathematics subject covers various mathematical concepts and skills such as algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and statistics.The goal of the subject is to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematical concepts and to develop their ability to think logically and analytically. It considered essential for students who plan to pursue further studies in fields such as science, engineering, and economics

Pendidikan Islam

focus of the course is on the study of Islamic principles and values and how to put them into practise. It covers a wide range of subjects, including the history and evolution of Islamic civilisation, the practises and beliefs of Islam, and the place of Islam in modern society. The student will be teach on how to answer KBAT questions, learn more about Tajweed question and completed a verse in Quran.
