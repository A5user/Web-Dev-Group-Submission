About Us

Our mission is aligned with the SDG goals proposed by the United Nations, especially SDG 4 QUALITY EDUACTION TO ALL. Our courses are for everyone from all backgrounds and people. For those who cannot afford the price, you can fill out the form below to access the course for free. Globalsadaqah is a legitimate charitable organization that conducts donations for those in need. Approximately 50% of purchases received are donated to Globalsadaqah. We also have a 100% donation policy for those who wish to donate.

We chose e-learning as the topic of our group project. Our group consists of 5 members. Our projects are linked to the goals of the SDGs, in particular SDG-4, which aims to provide quality education for all. Our website was created on December 28, 2020.
